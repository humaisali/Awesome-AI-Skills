## Overview

The **Business & Marketing Skills** category transforms your AI agent into a seasoned business consultant, growth marketer, and product strategist. Instead of generic advice, these skills inject proven frameworks, industry standards, and data-driven methodologies directly into your AI's reasoning capabilities. Whether you are crafting a comprehensive go-to-market strategy, optimizing your website for search engines, or managing complex product lifecycles, this collection ensures your AI delivers actionable, high-impact results aligned with modern business practices.

---

## Subcategories

This category contains the following specialized domains:

### Business & Sales
Empower your agent with proven tactics for B2B and B2C sales, complex negotiation frameworks, lead generation strategies, and corporate strategy formulation. Use these skills to analyze market trends, draft compelling sales pitches, build robust business models, and evaluate competitive landscapes. The skills in this folder are designed to enforce data-driven decision-making and professional communication standards.

### Marketing & SEO
Inject deep knowledge of search engine optimization, content marketing, digital ad campaigns, and growth hacking into your AI. Your AI will be equipped to perform technical keyword research, audit website structures, write SEO-optimized copy, and structure marketing funnels effectively. This subcategory ensures your marketing collateral is not only persuasive but technically aligned with current search engine algorithms.

### Product Management
Turn your agent into an experienced Product Owner and Manager. These skills cover agile methodologies, user story creation, backlog grooming, competitive analysis, and strategic product roadmap planning. By utilizing these modules, you can guide your AI to define minimum viable products (MVPs), track key performance indicators (KPIs), and align engineering outputs with user needs and business objectives.

---

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the subcategory that matches your current business objective.
2. Browse the folders to locate the specific skill module you require.
3. Attach or copy the contents of the `SKILL.md` file into your agent's context (for example, into Claude, Antigravity, or Cursor).
4. Initiate your prompt and watch your agent execute tasks with domain-expert precision, constrained by the best practices defined in the skill.
