## Overview

The **System Design & UI-UX Skills** category focuses on the critical intersection of high-level architectural planning and the art of human-computer interaction. While AI models naturally excel at logical problem-solving, these skills provide the crucial guidelines needed for designing scalable macro-systems as well as beautiful, accessible, and highly functional user interfaces. Use these modules to guide your agent in wireframing, component design, typography selection, user journey mapping, and overall structural design.

---

## Subcategories

### UI & UX Design
This subcategory is packed with specialized skills that teach your AI how to think like a seasoned UX Researcher and UI Designer. It covers core usability principles, color theory application, stringent accessibility standards (WCAG compliance), responsive design systems, and the implementation of subtle micro-interactions. Apply these skills to ensure your applications are not just technically sound, but visually cohesive and delightful for the end-user.

---

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the subcategory that matches your design or architectural focus.
2. Browse the folders to locate the specific skill module you require.
3. Attach or copy the contents of the `SKILL.md` file into your agent's context (for example, into Claude, Antigravity, or Cursor).
4. Initiate your prompt and watch your agent execute tasks with domain-expert precision, constrained by the best practices defined in the skill.
